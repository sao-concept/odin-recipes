Chicken Pot Pie

Chicken Pot Pie is a comforting and hearty dish that combines tender chicken, colorful vegetables, and a creamy filling, all encased in a flaky pastry crust.

This classic comfort food brings together the flavors and textures that make it a beloved favorite.

Ingredients

For the crepes:

  • 2 store-bought or homemade pie crusts

For the filling:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, diced or shredded
  • 1 cup carrots, diced
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 1/2 cup celery, diced
  • 1/2 cup onion, diced
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup milk or cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Herbs and seasonings of choice (such as thyme, parsley, or rosemary)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a large skillet or pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the diced onions, carrots, celery, and any other desired vegetables. Sauté until the vegetables are tender.
  3. Stir in the all-purpose flour, coating the vegetables evenly. Cook for about a minute to cook off the raw flour taste.
  4. Slowly pour in the chicken broth and milk or cream, stirring constantly. Continue cooking and stirring until the mixture thickens and comes to a simmer. This will create the creamy filling for the pot pie.
  5. Add the cooked chicken, peas, and any herbs or seasonings of your choice. Stir to combine and cook for a few more minutes until everything is heated through. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Remove the skillet or pot from heat and set it aside to cool slightly.
  7. Prepare a pie dish or baking dish by lining it with one of the pie crusts.
  8. Pour the chicken and vegetable filling into the prepared crust.
  9. Cover the filling with the second pie crust. You can choose to keep it whole, create a lattice pattern, or cut it into shapes for decoration. Seal the edges by crimping them together with a fork.
  10. Make a few small slits on the top crust to allow steam to escape during baking.
  11. Place the pot pie in the preheated oven and bake for approximately 30-35 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbly.
  12. Remove the pot pie from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
