Easy French Dip Sandwiches

Easy French Dip Sandwiches are a satisfying and savory meal that combines tender, thinly sliced roast beef with crusty bread and a flavorful au jus dipping sauce.

This classic sandwich is effortless to prepare, making it a perfect choice for a quick and delicious lunch or dinner option.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound thinly sliced roast beef
  • 4-6 crusty French rolls or baguettes
  • 8-12 slices of provolone or Swiss cheese
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Butter or oil for sautéing

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a saucepan, heat some butter or oil over medium heat. Add the sliced onions and sauté until they become soft and caramelized.
  3. In a separate saucepan, combine the beef broth, minced garlic, and Worcestershire sauce. Bring the mixture to a simmer and let it cook for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Split the French rolls or baguettes lengthwise and lay them on a baking sheet. If desired, lightly toast them in the preheated oven for a few minutes.
  5. Arrange the thinly sliced roast beef on one side of each roll. Top the roast beef with slices of provolone or Swiss cheese.
  6. Place the sandwich halves back in the oven to melt the cheese. Bake for about 5 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Once the cheese has melted, remove the sandwich halves from the oven and carefully press the top and bottom halves together.
  8. Slice the sandwiches into individual servings.
  9. Serve the French Dip Sandwiches with small bowls of the warm au jus sauce for dipping.
  10. Enjoy your Easy French Dip Sandwiches while they are still warm and the cheese is gooey.
